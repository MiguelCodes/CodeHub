Features

Secured

Using helmet.js as middleware, we secure your data as much as possible by removing headers with sensitive info and providing XSS protection.

Public Scripts

We make free scripts for you and for the whole community! Including markdown! Speaking of markdown, our Codelet `*.md` files also support markdown.

Mobile Friendly

CodeHub was tested hundreds of times on mobile devices (tablets, phones, etc.) and the latest test is works for the tested iPhone XR, and the Samsung Galaxy S9. It might work on your device!

We Listen

As a victim user of a website and reporting with no response, we really know what it feels like. Getting the feeling of hackers upon you. But our team listens to all your reports and sends back a response.
